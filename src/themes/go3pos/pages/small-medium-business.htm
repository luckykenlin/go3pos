Small & Medium Business

1. Targeted Marketing

Keeping your customers loyal is facilitated by Novotill CRM and Marketing features, thanks to the accurate profile building, as well as customizable rewards schemes. The system collects data in a comprehensive manner via QR E-Menu, payment and Social Media

2. Tablet POS

Novotill Tablet POS System makes it easier and faster than ever to run your business in one device. Built-in capabilities allow you to simplify daily tasks, speed up transactions, engage consumers and accept the latest payments effortlessly.

  • Multi-task like a pro with 4GB or 8GB of RAM that can run multiple apps and enable faster transactions.
  • A 15.6-inch, HD display and double-screen option for more direct consumer engagement.
  • Peripheral Enabled: From barcode/QR code scanners and weight scales to kitchen printers and more, Novotill tablet POS is compatible with a variety of peripherals.
  • Novotill tablet POS works seamlessly with all Novotill family devices to support transactions at the counter, in line or on the go.
  • With built-in Novotill apps for restaurants, you can manage your daily operation in a click—seat guests, manage tables, take orders, call for queuing numbers and accept payments.

3. Handheld/Mobile POS

Small in size, complete in function. Novotill handheld/mobile POS is designed to set your business free from complicated hardware installation and maintenance. Novotill handheld/mobile POS is the all-in-one mobile POS device with the built-in capabilities to help you serve customers better—whether at the counter, in line, at the table or in the salon chair.

  • Build effective loyalty programs, collect customer feedback and send customer promos in minutes.
  • Everything you need - Embedded printer, barcode/QR code scanner and camera.
  • Do business anywhere – with Wifi or offline.

4. Back office

Monitor real time sales data and manage employee permissions and shifts from anywhere through Novotill Manager app and web dashboard. Integrated with purchase data from consumers and operation data from the restaurant, you can check real-time performance evaluation with D Manager – from Profit&Loss Analysis to Table Turnover, from Ingredient Consumption to Customer Flow. Our reporting solutions empower you to utilize data from various perspectives and analytics to manage and help you identify sales trends and seize opportunities for growth. Furthermore, you can make data-oriented decisions on a wide range of subjects that impacts operations of the restaurant. For example, if you seek advices on location of a new shop, Novotill can offer a comprehensive and accurate analysis based on competition status, infrastructure, trends, best-practice etc. It’s also easier than ever to get the funding to scale up your business with Novotill. Thanks to accurate business data collection, Novotill’s partner financial institutions can help you by turning your future sales into working capital. Imagine what that could mean for your business. You can upgrade or purchase new equipment, make renovations, expand your business, purchase inventory and much more.
